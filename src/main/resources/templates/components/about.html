About Paani Foundation

Paani Foundation is a non-profit organization established in 2016 with the mission to make Maharashtra drought-free through community participation and scientific water conservation techniques.

Founded by Satyajit Bhatkal and the team behind the TV show Satyamev Jayate, Paani Foundation works to empower rural communities to solve their own water problems through the annual Water Cup competition and year-round training programs.
