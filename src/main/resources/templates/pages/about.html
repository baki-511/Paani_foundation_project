Mission & Vision

Our Mission

To make Maharashtra drought-free by empowering rural communities with water conservation techniques and sustainable watershed management.

Our Vision

A Maharashtra where every village is water-sufficient, ecosystems are thriving, and communities are resilient to climate change.

Our Approach

Community-driven solutions, scientific watershed development, and large-scale public participation through the Water Cup competition.
